At [the Children's Learning Lab](https://peabody.vanderbilt.edu/academics/departments/psych/research-labs/childrens-learning-lab/), I am supervised by [Bethany Rittle-Johnson](https://www.vanderbilt.edu/psychological_sciences/bio/bethany-rittle-johnson). I serve as the graduate student lead on an NSF project, *Leveraging Numerical Patterns in a Modified Board Game*. Our research examines how different hundred board layouts and gameplay variations influence learners’ numeracy development, especially place value knowledge.

![image](/images/NP.png)

My master’s thesis focuses on the impact of calculator use on learners’ understanding of the equal sign.
